Start

Split into teams. Each round, a team selects a “Professor” from its group. The Professor clicks the button to determine what he is a professor of. The professor then stands before all players of opposing teams. The latter group is called “The Examiners.”

Play

The Professor must answer any question put forth by the examiners for 90 seconds.

Lose

The Professor loses if at any point she

  • Hesitates
  • Contradicts herself
  • Misspeaks or Stumbles verbally
  • Fails to answer the question
  • Laughs
  • Says "um"
